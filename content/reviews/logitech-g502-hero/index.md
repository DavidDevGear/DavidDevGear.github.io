## Overview

The **Logitech G502 HERO** is one of the most popular wired gaming mice of the last decade, known for its precision sensor, programmability, and ergonomic shape. 


<br>

## Design & Ergonomics

It’s built for **right-handed users**, with a thumb rest and comfortable grip, making it suitable for long sessions — whether gaming or designing. The adjustable weights let you customize the feel for your hand and playstyle. 


<br>

## Performance

The next-generation **HERO sensor** delivers up to 25,600 DPI tracking with minimal smoothing or filtering, ensuring precise and responsive input — ideal for competitive gaming. 



<br>

## Pros

- Excellent tracking and low latency  
- Highly customizable buttons and profiles  
- Strong build quality  




<br>
<br>

## Cons

- Wired only (no wireless option)  
- Might feel large for small hands  
- Cable can be stiff


<br>
<br>

## Best Use Cases

- FPS, MMO gaming  
- Office and productivity (scroll wheel free-scroll mode)
- Designers wanting precise pointing
