## Overview

The **LG UltraGear 27\"** ( 27GL850-B / 27GP850-B series) is a well-rounded 1440p gaming monitor with **excellent motion performance and vivid color quality**. 


<br>

## Performance
Users and experts consistently praise its **165 Hz refresh rate** and **1ms response times**, providing smooth gameplay and low input lag — valuable for both competitive gamers and creators. 



<br>

## Pros

- Sharp QHD resolution and vibrant Nano IPS panel  
- Great motion clarity and low lag  
- G-SYNC / FreeSync support  


<br>
<br>

## Cons

- HDR is basic  
- Contrast and blacks not the deepest  
- Stand may feel stiff to adjust


<br>
<br>

### Who It’s For

Gamers who want a **fast, versatile 27\" monitor** that can handle shooters, RPGs, and daily productivity — all without huge cost.




*Estimated Price Range:* ~$350 – $450  
*Best For:* 1440p gaming and creative work
