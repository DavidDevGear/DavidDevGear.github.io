## Overview

The **Keychron K10 Pro** is a full-size mechanical keyboard known for its **hot-swappable switches, QMK/VIA programmability, and multi-device support**. 


<br>

## Features

- Hot-swappable switch support — swap switches without soldering  
- Bluetooth + wired modes  
- QMK/VIA customizable layouts  
- 1000 Hz polling rate and responsive typing feel  


<br>
<br>


## Drawback

Some community feedback suggests **occasional quality control issues and customer support limitations**, common in affordable custom keyboards. 


<br>

## Verdict

Excellent for development and gaming workflows if you value **programmability and typing feel**, but expect to manage minor quirks.
